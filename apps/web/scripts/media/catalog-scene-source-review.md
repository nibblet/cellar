# Catalog Scene Source Review

Working notes for `generate-catalog-scenes.ts`.

Rule of thumb:
- `allow`: one bottle, mostly full bottle visible, label readable, low ambiguity.
- `borderline`: one bottle but angle/crop/props may reduce fidelity.
- `skip`: multiple bottles, review-card composites, partial bottle crops, or not really a bottle shot.
