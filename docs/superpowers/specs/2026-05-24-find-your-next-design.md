## Behavior

Below Winston's daily pairing card, a **Find your next** section offers three actions:

| Button | Opens | Suggestions |
|---|---|---|
| Today's Pairing | Sheet | Up to 5 cigar+bourbon pairs: **Have shelf first**, then preference/catalog pairs |
| Today's Pour | Sheet | Up to 5 bourbons: **Have first**, then catalog matches |
| Today's Smoke | Sheet | Up to 5 cigars: **Have first**, then catalog matches |

Each row links to `/pairings/[cigar]/[bourbon]` or `/products/[id]`. Source badge: "On your shelf" vs "From the catalog".
